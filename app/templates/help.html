How it works

A quick tour of what you can do here and how to get started. {% if not user %}Create an account to use the personal features.{% endif %}

For residents

1. Browse schemes

See and search government schemes — no login needed.

Browse schemes

2. Create your account

Sign up with your mobile number and a password. You can change the password later under Account.

{% if user %}Your account{% else %}Sign up{% endif %}

3. Fill your profile (once)

Add your details and ward. It's a one-time profile; later edits go to an admin for approval, and your current profile stays active until then.

{% if user %}Edit profile{% endif %}

4. See schemes you qualify for

Based on your profile, we instantly list every scheme you are eligible for, and which documents you still need.

{% if user %}My schemes{% endif %}

5. Keep your documents

Upload your documents once (number + a photo/scan). An admin approves them, then they're reused for every scheme. You can view or download them anytime.

{% if user %}My documents{% endif %}

6. Raise & track complaints

Report a civic issue (water, roads, garbage…). The public board shows it anonymously; you can track its status and withdraw it while it's still new.

Complaints board

7. Know who's responsible

See the village officials for each ward and service, with their contact details.

Officials

8. Share your profile

From your profile you can share your details and approved documents on WhatsApp with one tap.

{% if user %}My profile{% endif %}
